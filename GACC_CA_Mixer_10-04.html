German American Business Mixer

We are proud to say that due to our German American networking efforts, German Bratwurst by Johannes' Brats will be a new permanent addition to the menu at the American Alchemy Restaurant. Congratulations to both of you!

Join us to celebrate this kick-off event and do your own networking with German American business people.

Lunch with the German American Chamber of Commerce California offers a platform for everybody interested in German American relations to network, socialize, exchange ideas and knowledge, and just to get to know different companies and people.

At the same time, get information about the Business Program and the Career Program of the German American Chamber of Commerce California, and about our membership benefits.
  

Johannes' Brats. A taste of Germany in every bite.

are made in the authentic German tradition--without the shortcuts that mass production always brings. Our brats haven't been adapted to American tastes--or sausage-making techniques--like most many of the brats sold here. The American palette is ready for the real thing but the bratwurst wasn't here yet. Unleash your inner sausage expert. Go to a Germany of the mind. The main ingredients of Johannes' Brats are pork, beef, and natural spices imported from Germany. No chemicals or preservatives are ever used.

  

al•che•my [al-kuh-mee] n.

a process of transmuting a common substance, usually of little value, into a substance of great value. An amalgamation of globally influenced fare and the eclectic neighborhood restaurant and lounge, Alchemy is fast making a name for itself as a comfortable space to enjoy a palette pleasing selection of sophisticated dishes, unique cocktails and a casual yet contemporary ambiance. Anchored in the hip up-and-coming neighborhood of South Park along the burgeoning 30th Street 'restaurant row,' Alchemy provides restaurant-goers an authentic and sophisticated gastronomic experience at a surprisingly affordable price.
